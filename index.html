Who am I?

Hi, I'm Vijay G. First and foremost, I love writing code. Ever since, writing my first program in C and manipulating it to produce a desired output. I have been obsessed with the idea of using software to solve practical problems. Software Engineering is a never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I believe in the power of programming to transform and improve the lives of people around the world. [I want to be like Elon Musk, WHY? Because, I just love his ideas and the technology he uses to make Earth a better place]. Earlier I used to think how does a computer works? Is it Magic? Sounds funny, right? But, majority of humans, in their childhood have thought the same like me.
